avś_20.108.01.

त्वं॒ न इन्द्रा॒ भरं ओ॒जो नृम्णं॒ शतक्रतो विचर्षणे ।
आ॒ वीरं॒ पृतनाष॒हम् ॥१॥

tváṃ na indrā́ bharam̐ ójo nr̥mṇáṃ śatakrato vicarṣaṇe |
ā́ vīráṃ pr̥tanāṣáham ||1||

O Indra, bring great strength to us, bring valour, Satakratu, thou
most active, bring.
A hero conquering in war.

avś_20.108.02.

त्वं॒ हि॒ नः पिता॒ वसो त्वं॒ माता॒ शतक्रतो बभू॒विथ ।
अ॒धा ते सुम्न॒म् ईमहे ॥२॥

tváṃ hí naḥ pitā́ vaso tváṃ mātā́ śatakrato babhū́vitha |
ádhā te sumnám īmahe ||2||

For, gracious Satakratu, thou hast ever been a mother and a
sire to us,
So now for bliss we pray to thee.

avś_20.108.03.

त्वां॒ शुष्मिन् पुरुहूत वाजय॒न्तम् उ॒प ब्रुवे शतक्रतो ।
स॒ नो रास्व सुवी॒र्यम् ॥३॥

tvā́ṃ śuṣmin puruhūta vājayántam úpa bruve śatakrato |
sá no rāsva suvī́ryam ||3||

To thee, Strong, Much-invoked who showest forth thy strength,
O Satakratu, do I speak:
So grant thou us heroic might.
