avś_19.069.01.

जीवा॒ स्थ जीव्या॒सं स॒र्वम् आ॒युर् जीव्यासम् ॥१॥

jīvā́ stha jīvyā́saṃ sárvam ā́yur jīvyāsam ||1||

Ye are alive. I fain would live. I fain would live my complete
term of life.

avś_19.069.02.

उपजीवा॒ स्थो॒प जीव्यासं स॒र्वम् आ॒युर् जीव्यासम् ॥२॥

upajīvā́ sthópa jīvyāsaṃ sárvam ā́yur jīvyāsam ||2||

Ye live dependent. I fain would live dependent. I fain would
live my complete term of life.

avś_19.069.03.

संजीवा॒ स्थ सं॒ जीव्यासं स॒र्वम् आ॒युर् जीव्यासम् ॥३॥

saṃjīvā́ stha sáṃ jīvyāsaṃ sárvam ā́yur jīvyāsam ||3||

Ye remain alive. I fain would remain alive. I fain would live
my complete term of life.

avś_19.069.04.

जीवला॒ स्थ जीव्या॒सं स॒र्वम् आ॒युर् जीव्यासम् ॥४॥

jīvalā́ stha jīvyā́saṃ sárvam ā́yur jīvyāsam ||4||

Ye are life-givers. I fain would live. I fain would live my com-
plete term of life.
