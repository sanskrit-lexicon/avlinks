avś_20.033.01.

अप्सु॒ धूत॒स्य हरिवः पि॒बेह॒ नृ॒भिः सुत॒स्य जठ॒रं पृणस्व ।
मिमिक्षु॒र् य॒म् अ॒द्रय इन्द्र तु॒भ्यं ते॒भिर् वर्धस्व म॒दम् उक्थवाहः ॥१॥

apsú dhūtásya harivaḥ píbehá nŕ̥bhiḥ sutásya jaṭháraṃ pr̥ṇasva |
mimikṣúr yám ádraya indra túbhyaṃ tébhir vardhasva mádam ukthavāhaḥ ||1||

Drink of the juice which men have washed in waters and fill the-
full, O Lord of tawny horses.
O Indra, hearer of the laud, with Soma which stones have mix-
ed for thee enhance thy rapture.

avś_20.033.02.

प्रो॒ग्रां॒ पीतिं॒ वृ॒ष्ण इयर्मि सत्यां॒ प्रयै॒ सुत॒स्य हर्यश्व तु॒भ्यम् ।
इ॒न्द्र धे॒नाभिर् इह॒ मादयस्व धीभि॒र् वि॒श्वाभिः श॒च्या गृणानः॒ ॥२॥

prógrā́ṃ pītíṃ vŕ̥ṣṇa iyarmi satyā́ṃ prayái sutásya haryaśva túbhyam |
índra dhénābhir ihá mādayasva dhībhír víśvābhiḥ śácyā gr̥ṇānáḥ ||2||

To make thee start, a strong true draught I offer to thee the
Bull, O thou whom bay steeds carry.
Here take delight, O Indra, in our voices while thou art hymned
with power and all our spirit.

avś_20.033.03.

ऊती॒ शचीवस् त॒व वीर्ये॑ण व॒यो द॒धाना उशि॒ज ऋतज्ञाः॒ ।
प्रजा॒वद् इन्द्र मं॒सो दुरोणे॒ तस्थु॒र् गृण॒न्तः सधमा॒द्यासः ॥३॥

ūtī́ śacīvas táva vīryèṇa váyo dádhānā uśíja r̥tajñā́ḥ |
prajā́vad indra máṃso duroṇé tasthúr gr̥ṇántaḥ sadhamā́dyāsaḥ ||3||

O mighty Indra, through thine aid, thy prowess, obtaining life,
zealous, and skilled in worship.
Men in the house who share the sacred banquet stand singing
praise that brings them store of children.
