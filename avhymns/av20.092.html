avś_20.092.01.

अभि॒ प्र॒ गो॒पतिं गिरे॒न्द्रम् अर्च य॒था विदे॒ ।
सूतुं॒ सत्य॒स्य स॒त्पतिम् ॥१॥

abhí prá gópatiṃ giréndram arca yáthā vidé |
sūtúṃ satyásya sátpatim ||1||

Praise, even as he is known, with song Indra the guardian of the
kine,
The Son of Truth, Lord of the brave.

avś_20.092.02.

आ॒ ह॒रयः ससृज्रिरे॒ ऽरुषीर् अ॒धि बर्हि॒षि ।
य॒त्राभि॒ संन॒वामहे ॥२॥

ā́ hárayaḥ sasr̥jriré 'ruṣīr ádhi barhíṣi |
yátrābhí saṃnávāmahe ||2||

Hither his bay steeds have been sent, red steeds are on the sacred
grass.
Where we in concert sing our songs.

avś_20.092.03.

इ॒न्द्राय गा॒व आशि॒रं दुदुह्रे॒ वज्रि॒णे म॒धु ।
य॒त् सीम् उपह्वरे॒ विद॒त् ॥३॥

índrāya gā́va āśíraṃ duduhré vajríṇe mádhu |
yát sīm upahvaré vidát ||3||

For Indra thunder-armed the kine have yielded mingled milk
and meath.
What time he found them in the vault.

avś_20.092.04.

उ॒द् य॒द् ब्रध्न॒स्य विष्ट॒पं गृह॒म् इ॒न्द्रश् च ग॒न्वहि ।
म॒ध्वः पीत्वा॒ सचेवहि त्रिः॒ सप्त॒ स॒ख्युः पदे॒ ॥४॥

úd yád bradhnásya viṣṭápaṃ gr̥hám índraś ca gánvahi |
mádhvaḥ pītvā́ sacevahi tríḥ saptá sákhyuḥ padé ||4||

When I and Indra amount on high up to the bright One's place
and home,
We, having drunk of meath, will reach his seat whose Friends
are three-times-seven.

avś_20.092.05.

अ॒र्चत प्रा॒र्चत प्रि॒यमेधासो अ॒र्चत ।
अ॒र्चन्तु पुत्रका॒ उत॒ पु॒रं न॒ धृष्ण्व् अ॑र्चत ॥५॥

árcata prā́rcata príyamedhāso árcata |
árcantu putrakā́ utá púraṃ ná dhr̥ṣṇv àrcata ||5||

Sing, sing ye forth your songs of praise, ye Priyamedhas, sing
your songs:
Yea, let young children sing their lauds: as a strong castle praise
ye him.

avś_20.092.06.

अ॒व स्वराति ग॒र्गरो गोधा॒ प॒रि सनिष्वणत् ।
पि॒ङ्गा प॒रि चनिष्कदद् इ॒न्द्राय ब्र॒ह्मो॒द्यतम् ॥६॥

áva svarāti gárgaro godhā́ pári saniṣvaṇat |
píṅgā pári caniṣkadad índrāya bráhmódyatam ||6||

Now loudly let the viol sound, the lute send out its voice with
might,
Shrill be the music of the string. To Indra is the hymn upraised.

avś_20.092.07.

आ॒ य॒त् प॒तन्त्य् एन्यः॑ सुदु॒घा अ॒नपस्फुरः ।
अपस्फु॒रं गृभायत सो॒मम् इ॒न्द्राय पा॒तवे ॥७॥

ā́ yát pátanty enyàḥ sudúghā ánapasphuraḥ |
apasphúraṃ gr̥bhāyata sómam índrāya pā́tave ||7||

When hither speed the dappled cows, unflinching, easy to be
milked,
Seize quickly, as it bursts away, the Soma juice for Indra's
drink.

avś_20.092.08.

अ॒पाद् इ॒न्द्रो अ॒पाद् अग्नि॒र् वि॒श्वे देवा॒ अमत्सत ।
व॒रुण इ॒द् इह॒ क्षयत् त॒म् आ॒पो अभ्य् अ॑नूषत वत्सं॒ संशि॒श्वरीर् इव ॥८॥

ápād índro ápād agnír víśve devā́ amatsata |
váruṇa íd ihá kṣayat tám ā́po abhy ànūṣata vatsáṃ saṃśíśvarīr iva ||8||

Indra hath drunk; Agni hath drunk all Deities have drunk their
fill.
Here Varuna shall have his home, to whom the floods have sung
aloud as mother-kine unto their calves.

avś_20.092.09.

सुदेवो॒ असि वरुण य॒स्य ते सप्त॒ सि॒न्धवः ।
अनुक्ष॒रन्ति काकु॒दं सूर्यं॑ सुषिरा॒म् इव ॥९॥

sudevó asi varuṇa yásya te saptá síndhavaḥ |
anukṣáranti kākúdaṃ sūryàṃ suṣirā́m iva ||9||

Thou, Varuna, to whom belong the Seven Streams, art a glorious
God.
The waters flow into thy throat as'twere a pipe with ample
mouth.

avś_20.092.10.

यो॒ व्य॒तींर् अ॒फाणयत् सु॒युक्तां उ॒प दाशु॒षे ।
तक्वो॒ नेता॒ त॒द् इ॒द् व॒पुर् उपमा॒ यो॒ अ॒मुच्यत ॥१०॥

yó vyátīm̐r áphāṇayat súyuktām̐ úpa dāśúṣe |
takvó netā́ tád íd vápur upamā́ yó ámucyata ||10||

He who hath made the fleet steeds spring, well-harnessed, to the
worshipper,
He, the swift guide, is that fair form thot loosed the horses near
at hand.

avś_20.092.11.

अ॒ती॒द् उ शक्र॒ ओहत इ॒न्द्रो वि॒श्वा अ॒ति द्वि॒षः ।
भिन॒त् कनी॒न ओदनं॒ पच्य॒मानं परो॒ गिरा॒ ॥११॥

átī́d u śakrá ohata índro víśvā áti dvíṣaḥ |
bhinát kanī́na odanáṃ pacyámānaṃ paró girā́ ||11||

Indra, the very mighty, holds his enemies in utter scorn.
He, far away, and yet a child, cleft the cloud smitten by his
voice.

avś_20.092.12.

अर्भको॒ न॒ कुमारको॒ ऽधि तिष्ठन्न् अ॒वं र॒थम् ।
स॒ पक्षन् महिषं॒ मृगं॒ पित्रे॒ मात्रे॒ विभुक्र॒तुम् ॥१२॥

arbhakó ná kumārakó 'dhi tiṣṭhann ávaṃ rátham |
sá pakṣan mahiṣáṃ mr̥gáṃ pitré mātré vibhukrátum ||12||

He, yet a boy exceeding small, mounted his newly-fashioned car.
He for his Mother and his Sire cooked the wild mighty buffalo.

avś_20.092.13.

आ॒ तू॒ सुशिप्र दंपते र॒थं तिष्ठा हिरण्य॒यम् ।
अ॒ध द्युक्षं॒ सचेवहि सह॒स्रपादम् अरुषं॒ स्वस्तिगा॒म् अनेह॒सम् ॥१३॥

ā́ tū́ suśipra daṃpate ráthaṃ tiṣṭhā hiraṇyáyam |
ádha dyukṣáṃ sacevahi sahásrapādam aruṣáṃ svastigā́m anehásam ||13||

Lord of the Home, with beauteous cheeks, ascend thy chariot
wrought of gold.
We will attend the heavenly One; the thousand-footed, red of
hue, matchless, who blesses where he goes.

avś_20.092.14.

त॒म् घेम् इत्था॒ नमस्वि॒न उ॒प स्वरा॒जम् आसते ।
अ॒र्थं चिद् अस्य सु॒धितं य॒द् ए॒तवे आवर्त॒यन्ति दाव॒ने ॥१४॥

tám ghem itthā́ namasvína úpa svarā́jam āsate |
árthaṃ cid asya súdhitaṃ yád étave āvartáyanti dāváne ||14||

With reverence they come hitherward to him as to a sovran lord,
That they may bring him near for this man's good success, to
prosper and bestow his gifts.

avś_20.092.15.

अ॒नु प्रत्न॒स्यौ॒कसः प्रिय॒मेधास एषाम् ।
पू॒र्वाम् अ॒नु प्र॒यतिं वृक्त॒बर्हिषो हित॒प्रयस आशत ॥१५॥

ánu pratnásyáukasaḥ priyámedhāsa eṣām |
pū́rvām ánu práyatiṃ vr̥ktábarhiṣo hitáprayasa āśata ||15||

The Priyamedhas have observed the offering of the men of old,
Of ancient custom, while they strewed the sacred grass and
spread their sacrificial food.

avś_20.092.16.

यो॒ रा॒जा चर्षणीनां॒ या॒ता र॒थेभिर् अ॒ध्रिगुः ।
वि॒श्वासां तरुता॒ पृ॒तनानां ज्ये॒ष्ठो यो॒ वृत्रहा॒ गृणे॒ ॥१६॥

yó rā́jā carṣaṇīnā́ṃ yā́tā ráthebhir ádhriguḥ |
víśvāsāṃ tarutā́ pŕ̥tanānāṃ jyéṣṭho yó vr̥trahā́ gr̥ṇé ||16||

He who as sovran Lord of men moves with his chariots
unrestrained,
The Vritra-slayer, queller of all fighting hosts, preeminent, is
praised in song.

avś_20.092.17.

इ॒न्द्रं तं॒ शुम्भ पुरुहन्मन्न् अ॒वसे य॒स्य द्विता॒ विधर्त॒रि ।
ह॒स्ताय व॒ज्रः प्र॒ति धायि दर्शतो॒ महो॒ दिवे॒ न॒ सू॒र्यः ॥१७॥

índraṃ táṃ śumbha puruhanmann ávase yásya dvitā́ vidhartári |
hástāya vájraḥ práti dhāyi darśató mahó divé ná sū́ryaḥ ||17||

Honour that Indra, Puruhanman! for his aid, in whose sustain-
ing hand of old.
The splendid bolt of thunder was deposited, as the great Sun
was set in heaven.

avś_20.092.18.

न॒किष् टं॒ क॒र्मणा नशद् य॒श् चका॒र सदा॒वृधम् ।
इ॒न्द्रं न॒ यज्ञै॒र् विश्व॒गूर्तम् ऋ॒भ्वसम् अ॒धृष्टं धृष्ण्वो॑जसम् ॥१८॥

nákiṣ ṭáṃ kármaṇā naśad yáś cakā́ra sadā́vr̥dham |
índraṃ ná yajñáir viśvágūrtam ŕ̥bhvasam ádhr̥ṣṭaṃ dhr̥ṣṇvòjasam ||18||

No one by deed attains to him who works and strengthens
evermore:
No, not by sacrifice, to Indra praised of all, resistless, daring,
bold in might;

avś_20.092.19.

अ॒षाल्हम् उग्रं॒ पृ॒तनासु सासहिं॒ य॒स्मिन् मही॒र् उरुज्र॒यः ।
सं॒ धेन॒वो जा॒यमाने अनोनवुर् द्या॒वः क्षा॒मो अनोनवुः ॥१९॥

áṣālham ugráṃ pŕ̥tanāsu sāsahíṃ yásmin mahī́r urujráyaḥ |
sáṃ dhenávo jā́yamāne anonavur dyā́vaḥ kṣā́mo anonavuḥ ||19||

The potent Conqueror, invincible in war, him at whose birth
the mighty ones,
The kine who spread afar, sent their loud voices out, heavens,
earths sent their loud voices out.

avś_20.092.20.

य॒द् द्या॒व इन्द्र ते शतं॒ शतं॒ भू॒मीर् उत॒ स्युः॒ ।
न॒ त्वा वज्रिन्त् सह॒स्रं सू॒र्या अ॒नु न॒ जात॒म् अष्ट रो॒दसी ॥२०॥

yád dyā́va indra te śatáṃ śatáṃ bhū́mīr utá syúḥ |
ná tvā vajrint sahásraṃ sū́ryā ánu ná jātám aṣṭa ródasī ||20||

O Indra, if a hundred heavens and if a hundred earths were
thine
No, not a thousand suns could match thee at thy birth, not
both the worlds, O Thunderer.

avś_20.092.21.

आ॒ पप्राथ महिना॒ वृ॒ष्ण्या वृषन् वि॒श्वा शविष्ठ श॒वसा ।
अस्मां॒ अव मघवन् गो॒मति व्रजे॒ व॒ज्रिं चित्रा॒भिर् ऊति॒भिः ॥२१॥

ā́ paprātha mahinā́ vŕ̥ṣṇyā vr̥ṣan víśvā śaviṣṭha śávasā |
asmā́m̐ ava maghavan gómati vrajé vájriṃ citrā́bhir ūtíbhiḥ ||21||

Thou, Hero, hast performed thy hero deeds with might, yea, all
with strength, O Strongest One.
Maghavan, help us to a stable full of kine, O Thunderer, with
wondrous aids.
