avś_20.142.01.

अ॒भुत्स्य् उ प्र॒ देव्या॒ साकं॒ वाचा॒हम् अश्वि॒नोः ।
व्य् आ॑वर् देव्य् आ॒ मतिं॒ वि॒ रातिं॒ म॒र्त्येभ्यः ॥१॥

ábhutsy u prá devyā́ sākáṃ vācā́ham aśvínoḥ |
vy ā̀var devy ā́ matíṃ ví rātíṃ mártyebhyaḥ ||1||

Together with the Goddess, with the Asvins' Speech have I
awoke.
Thou, Goddess, hast disclosed the hymn and holy gift from
mortal men.

avś_20.142.02.

प्र॒ बोधयोषो अश्वि॒ना प्र॒ देवि सूनृते महि ।
प्र॒ यज्ञहोतर् आनुष॒क् प्र॒ म॒दाय श्र॒वो बृह॒त् ॥२॥

prá bodhayoṣo aśvínā prá devi sūnr̥te mahi |
prá yajñahotar ānuṣák prá mádāya śrávo br̥hát ||2||

Awake the Asvins, Goddess Dawn! Up, mighty Lady of Sweet
Strains!
Rise straightway, priest of sacrifice! High glory to the glad-
dening draught!

avś_20.142.03.

य॒द् उषो या॒सि भानु॒ना सं॒ सू॒र्येण रोचसे ।
आ॒ हाय॒म् अश्वि॒नो र॒थो वर्ति॒र् याति नृपा॒य्यम् ॥३॥

yád uṣo yā́si bhānúnā sáṃ sū́ryeṇa rocase |
ā́ hāyám aśvíno rátho vartír yāti nr̥pā́yyam ||3||

Thou, Dawn, approaching with thy light, shinest together with
the Sun,
And to this man-protecting home the chariot of the Asvins
comes.

avś_20.142.04.

य॒द् आ॒पीतासो अंश॒वो गा॒वो न॒ दुह्र॒ ऊ॒धभिः ।
य॒द् वा वा॒णीर् अ॒नुषत प्र॒ देवय॒न्तो अश्वि॒ना ॥४॥

yád ā́pītāso aṃśávo gā́vo ná duhrá ū́dhabhiḥ |
yád vā vā́ṇīr ánuṣata prá devayánto aśvínā ||4||

When yellow stalks give forth the juice as cows from udders
pour their milk,
And voices sound the song of praise, the Asvins' worshippers
show first.

avś_20.142.05.

प्र॒ द्युम्ना॒य प्र॒ श॒वसे प्र॒ नृषा॒ह्याय श॒र्मणे ।
प्र॒ द॒क्षाय प्रचेतसा ॥५॥

prá dyumnā́ya prá śávase prá nr̥ṣā́hyāya śármaṇe |
prá dákṣāya pracetasā ||5||

Forward for glory and for strength, protection that shall con-
quer men,
And power and skill, most sapient Ones!

avś_20.142.06.

य॒न् नूनं॒ धीभि॒र् अश्विना पितु॒र् यो॒ना निषी॒दथः ।
य॒द् वा सुम्ने॒भिर् उक्थ्या ॥६॥

yán nūnáṃ dhībhír aśvinā pitúr yónā niṣī́dathaḥ |
yád vā sumnébhir ukthyā ||6||

When, Asvins worthy of our lauds, ye seat you in the father's
house.
With wisdom or the bliss ye bring.
