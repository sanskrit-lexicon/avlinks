ब्र॒ह्मणा ते ब्रह्मयु॒जा युनज्मि ह॒री स॒खाया सधमा॒द आशू॒ ।
स्थिरं॒ र॒थं सुख॒म् इन्द्राधिति॒ष्ठन् प्रजान॒न् विद्वां॒ उ॒प याहि सो॒मम् ॥१॥

bráhmaṇā te brahmayújā yunajmi hárī sákhāyā sadhamā́da āśū́ |
sthiráṃ ráthaṃ sukhám indrādhitíṣṭhan prajānán vidvā́m̐ úpa yāhi sómam ||1||

Those who are yoked by prayer with prayer I harness, the two.
fleet friendly Bays who joy together.
Mounting thy firm and easy car, O Indra, wise and all-knowing
come thou to the Soma.

Translations compiled by Dr. Mārcis Gasūns.
