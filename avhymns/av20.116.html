avś_20.116.01.

मा॒ भूम नि॒ष्ट्या इवे॒न्द्र त्व॒द् अ॒रणा इव ।
व॒नानि नि॒ प्रजहिता॒न्य् अद्रिवो दुरो॒षासो अमन्महि ॥१॥

mā́ bhūma níṣṭyā ivéndra tvád áraṇā iva |
vánāni ní prajahitā́ny adrivo duróṣāso amanmahi ||1||

Never may we be cast aside and strangers, as it were to thee.
We, Thunder-wielding Indra, count ourselves as trees rejected
and unfit to burn.

avś_20.116.02.

अ॒मन्मही॒द् अनाश॒वो ऽनुग्रा॒सश् च वृत्रहन् ।
सुकृ॒त् सु॒ ते महता॒ शूर रा॒धसा॒नु स्तो॒मं मुदीमहि ॥२॥

ámanmahī́d anāśávo 'nugrā́saś ca vr̥trahan |
sukŕ̥t sú te mahatā́ śūra rā́dhasā́nu stómaṃ mudīmahi ||2||

O Vritra slayer, we were thought slow and unready for the fray:
Yet once in thy great bounty may we have delight, O Hero,
after praising thee.
