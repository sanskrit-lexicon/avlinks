avś_20.102.01.

ईले॒न्यो नमस्य॑स् तिर॒स् त॒मांसि दर्शतः॒ ।
स॒म् अग्नि॒र् इध्यते वृ॒षा ॥१॥

īlényo namasyàs tirás támāṃsi darśatáḥ |
sám agnír idhyate vŕ̥ṣā ||1||

Meet to be lauded and adored, showing in beauty through the=
dark,
Agni the Bull is kindled well,

avś_20.102.02.

वृ॒षो अग्निः॒ स॒म् इध्यते॒ ऽश्वो न॒ देववा॒हनः ।
तं॒ हवि॒ष्मन्तः ईलते ॥२॥

vŕ̥ṣo agníḥ sám idhyaté 'śvo ná devavā́hanaḥ |
táṃ havíṣmantaḥ īlate ||2||

Agni is kindled as a Bull, like a horse bearer of the Gods;
Men with oblations worship him.

avś_20.102.03.

वृ॒षणं त्वा वयं॒ वृषन् वृ॒षणः स॒म् इधीमहि ।
अ॒ग्ने दी॒द्यतं बृह॒त् ॥३॥

vŕ̥ṣaṇaṃ tvā vayáṃ vr̥ṣan vŕ̥ṣaṇaḥ sám idhīmahi |
ágne dī́dyataṃ br̥hát ||3||

Thee will we kindle as a Bull, we who are bulls ourselves, O
Bull,
Thee, Agni, shining mightily.

Translations compiled by Dr. Mārcis Gasūns.
