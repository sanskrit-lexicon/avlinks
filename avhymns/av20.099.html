avś_20.099.01.

अभि॒ त्वा पूर्व॒पीतय इ॒न्द्र स्तो॒मेभिर् आय॒वः ।
समीचीना॒स ऋभ॒वः स॒म् अस्वरन् रुद्रा॒ गृणन्त पू॒र्व्यम् ॥१॥

abhí tvā pūrvápītaya índra stómebhir āyávaḥ |
samīcīnā́sa r̥bhávaḥ sám asvaran rudrā́ gr̥ṇanta pū́rvyam ||1||

Men with their lauds are urging thee, Indra, to drink the Soma
first.
The Ribhus in accord have lifted up their voice and Rudras.
sung thee as the First.

avś_20.099.02.

अस्ये॒द् इ॒न्द्रो वावृधे वृ॒ष्ण्यं श॒वो म॒दे सुत॒स्य वि॒ष्णवि ।
अद्या॒ त॒म् अस्य महिमा॒नम् आय॒वो॒ ऽनु ष्टुवन्ति पूर्व॒था ॥२॥

asyéd índro vāvr̥dhe vŕ̥ṣṇyaṃ śávo máde sutásya víṣṇavi |
adyā́ tám asya mahimā́nam āyávó 'nu ṣṭuvanti pūrváthā ||2||

Indra increased his manly strength at sacrifice, in the wild
rapture of this juice;
And living men to-day, even as of old, sing forth their praises to
his majesty.
