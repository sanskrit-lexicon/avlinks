avś_20.083.01.

इ॒न्द्र त्रिधा॒तु शरणं॒ त्रिव॒रूथं स्वस्तिम॒त् ।
छर्दि॒र् यछ मघ॒वद्भ्यश् च म॒ह्यं च याव॒या दिद्यु॒म् एभ्यः ॥१॥

índra tridhā́tu śaraṇáṃ trivárūthaṃ svastimát |
chardír yacha maghávadbhyaś ca máhyaṃ ca yāváyā didyúm ebhyaḥ ||1||

O Indra, grant a happy home, a triple refuge, triply strong.
Bestow a dwelling-place on the rich lords and me, and keep thy
dart afar from these.

avś_20.083.02.

ये॒ गव्यता॒ म॒नसा श॒त्रुम् आदभु॒र् अभिप्रघ्न॒न्ति धृष्णुया॒ ।
अ॒घ स्मा नो मघवन्न् इन्द्र गिर्वणस् तनूपा॒ अ॒न्तमो भव ॥२॥

yé gavyatā́ mánasā śátrum ādabhúr abhipraghnánti dhr̥ṣṇuyā́ |
ágha smā no maghavann indra girvaṇas tanūpā́ ántamo bhava ||2||

They who with minds intent on spoil subdue the foe, boldly
attack and smite him down.
From these, O Indra, Bounteous Lord who lovest song, be
closest guardian of our lives.
