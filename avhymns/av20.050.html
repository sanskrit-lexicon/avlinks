avś_20.050.01.

क॒न् न॒व्यो अतसी॒नां तुरो॒ गृणीत म॒र्त्यः ।
नही॒ न्व् अ॑स्य महिमा॒नम् इन्द्रियं॒ स्व॑र् गृण॒न्त आनशुः॒ ॥१॥

kán návyo atasī́nāṃ turó gr̥ṇīta mártyaḥ |
nahī́ nv àsya mahimā́nam indriyáṃ svàr gr̥ṇánta ānaśúḥ ||1||

What newest of imploring hymns shall, then, the zealous mortal
sing?
For have not they who laud his might and Indra-power won for
themselves the light of heaven?

avś_20.050.02.

क॒द् उ स्तुव॒न्त ऋतयन्त देव॒त ऋ॒षिः को॒ वि॒प्र ओहते ।
कदा॒ ह॒वं मघवन्न् इन्द्र सुन्वतः॒ क॒द् उ स्तुवत॒ आ॒ गमः ॥२॥

kád u stuvánta r̥tayanta deváta ŕ̥ṣiḥ kó vípra ohate |
kadā́ hávaṃ maghavann indra sunvatáḥ kád u stuvatá ā́ gamaḥ ||2||

When shall they keep the Law and praise thee mid the Gods?
Who counts as Rishi and as sage?
When wilt thou ever, Indra, Bounteous Lord, come nigh to
presser's or to praiser's call?
