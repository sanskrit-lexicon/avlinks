avś_19.051.01.

अ॒युतो ऽह॒म् अ॒युतो म आत्मा॒युतं मे च॒क्षुर् अ॒युतं मे श्रो॒त्रम् ।
अ॒युतो मे प्राणो॒ ऽयुतो मे ऽपानो॒ ऽयुतो मे व्यानो॒ ऽयुतो ऽहं॒ स॒र्वः ॥१॥

áyuto 'hám áyuto ma ātmā́yutaṃ me cákṣur áyutaṃ me śrótram |
áyuto me prāṇó 'yuto me 'pānó 'yuto me vyānó 'yuto 'háṃ sárvaḥ ||1||

Undisturbed am I, undisturbed is my soul, undisturbed mine
eye, undisturbed mine ear, undisturbed is mine in-breathing,
undisturbed mine out-breathing, undisturbed my diffusive-
breath, undisturbed the whole of me.

avś_19.051.02.

देव॒स्य त्वा सवितुः॒ प्रसवे॒ ऽश्वि॒नोर् बाहु॒भ्यां पूष्णो॒ ह॒स्ताभ्यां प्र॒सूत आ॒ रभे ॥२॥

devásya tvā savitúḥ prasavé 'śvínor bāhúbhyāṃ pūṣṇó hástābhyāṃ prásūta ā́ rabhe ||2||

Under the impulse of the God Savitar, sent forth from the arms-
of the Asvins and both hands of Pūshan I have taken thee.
