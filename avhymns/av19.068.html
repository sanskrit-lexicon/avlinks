अ॒व्यसश् च व्य॒चसश् च बि॒लं वि॒ ष्यामि माय॒या ।
ता॒भ्याम् उद्धृ॒त्य वे॒दम् अ॒थ क॒र्माणि कृण्महे ॥१॥

ávyasaś ca vyácasaś ca bílaṃ ví ṣyāmi māyáyā |
tā́bhyām uddhŕ̥tya védam átha kármāṇi kr̥ṇmahe ||1||

Both of broad and narrow I with magic power unclose the
mouth.
With these when we have raised the bunch of grass we pay the
holy rites.

Translations compiled by Dr. Mārcis Gasūns.
