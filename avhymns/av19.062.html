avś_19.062.01.

प्रियं॒ मा कृणु देवे॒षु प्रियं॒ रा॒जसु मा कृणु ।
प्रियं॒ स॒र्वस्य प॒श्यत उत॒ शूद्र॒ उता॒र्ये ॥१॥

priyáṃ mā kr̥ṇu devéṣu priyáṃ rā́jasu mā kr̥ṇu |
priyáṃ sárvasya páśyata utá śūdrá utā́rye ||1||

Make me beloved among the Gods, beloved among the Princes,
make
Me dear to everyone who sees, to Sūdra and to Aryan man.

Translations compiled by Dr. Mārcis Gasūns.
