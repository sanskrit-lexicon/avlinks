avś_20.124.01.

क॒या नश् चित्र॒ आ॒ भुवद् ऊती॒ सदा॒वृधः स॒खा ।
क॒या श॒चिष्ठया वृता॒ ॥१॥

káyā naś citrá ā́ bhuvad ūtī́ sadā́vr̥dhaḥ sákhā |
káyā śáciṣṭhayā vr̥tā́ ||1||

With what help will he come to us, wonderful, ever-waxing
Friend,
With what most mighty company?

avś_20.124.02.

क॒स् त्वा सत्यो॒ म॒दानां मं॒हिष्ठो मत्सद् अ॒न्धसः ।
दृल्हा॒ चिद् आरु॒जे व॒सु ॥२॥

kás tvā satyó mádānāṃ máṃhiṣṭho matsad ándhasaḥ |
dr̥lhā́ cid ārúje vásu ||2||

What genuine and most liberal draught will spirit thee with
juice to burst.
Open e'en strongly-guarded wealth?

avś_20.124.03.

अभी॒ षु॒ नः स॒खीनाम् अविता॒ जरितृ̄णा॒म् ।
शतं॒ भवास्य् ऊति॒भिः ॥३॥

abhī́ ṣú naḥ sákhīnām avitā́ jaritr̥̄ṇā́m |
śatáṃ bhavāsy ūtíbhiḥ ||3||

Do thou who art protector us thy friends who praise thee
With hundred aids approach us.

avś_20.124.04.

इमा॒ नु॒ कं भु॒वना सीषधामे॒न्द्रश् च वि॒श्वे च देवाः॒ ।
यज्ञं॒ च नस् तन्वं॑ च प्रजां॒ चादित्यै॒र् इ॒न्द्रः सह॒ चीक्ल्̥पाति ॥४॥

imā́ nú kaṃ bhúvanā sīṣadhāméndraś ca víśve ca devā́ḥ |
yajñáṃ ca nas tanvàṃ ca prajā́ṃ cādityáir índraḥ sahá cīkl̥pāti ||4||

We will, with Indra and all Gods to help us, bring these existing
worlds into subjection.
Our sacrifice, our bodies, and our offspring shall Indra form
together with the Ādityas.

avś_20.124.05.

आदित्यै॒र् इ॒न्द्रः स॒गणो मरु॒द्भिर् अस्मा॒कं भूत्व् अविता॒ तनू॒नाम् ।
हत्वा॒य देवा॒ अ॒सुरान् य॒द् आ॒यन् देवा॒ देवत्व॒म् अभिर॒क्षमाणाः ॥५॥

ādityáir índraḥ ságaṇo marúdbhir asmā́kaṃ bhūtv avitā́ tanū́nām |
hatvā́ya devā́ ásurān yád ā́yan devā́ devatvám abhirákṣamāṇāḥ ||5||

With the Ādityas, with the band of Maruts, may Indra be pro-
tector of our bodies.
As when the Gods came after they had slaughtered the Asuras,
keeping safe their Godlike nature,

avś_20.124.06.

प्रत्य॒ञ्चम् अर्क॒म् अनयं छ॒चीभिर् आ॒द् इ॒त् स्वधा॒म् इषिरा॒म् प॒र्य् अपश्यन् ।
अया॒ वा॒जं देव॒हितं सनेम म॒देम शत॒हिमाः सुवी॒राः ॥६॥

pratyáñcam arkám anayaṃ chácībhir ā́d ít svadhā́m iṣirā́m páry apaśyan |
ayā́ vā́jaṃ deváhitaṃ sanema mádema śatáhimāḥ suvī́rāḥ ||6||

Brought the Sun hitherward with mighty powers, and looked
about them on their vigorous Godhead.
With this may we obtain strength God-appointed, and joy with
brave sons through a hundred winters.
