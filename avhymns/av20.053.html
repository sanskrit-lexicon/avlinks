avś_20.053.01.

क॒ ईं वेद सुते॒ स॒चा पि॒बन्तं क॒द् व॒यो दधे ।
अयं॒ यः॒ पु॒रो विभिन॒त्त्य् ओ॒जसा मन्दानः॒ शिप्र्य् अ॒न्धसः ॥१॥

ká īṃ veda suté sácā píbantaṃ kád váyo dadhe |
ayáṃ yáḥ púro vibhinátty ójasā mandānáḥ śipry ándhasaḥ ||1||

Who knows what vital power he wins, drinking beside the
flowing juice?
This is the fair-cheeked God who, joying in the draught, breaks
down the castles in his strength.

avś_20.053.02.

दाना॒ मृगो॒ न॒ वारणः॒ पुरुत्रा॒ चर॒थं दधे ।
न॒किष् ट्वा नि॒ यमद् आ॒ सुते॒ गमो महा॒श् चरस्य् ओ॒जसा ॥२॥

dānā́ mr̥gó ná vāraṇáḥ purutrā́ caráthaṃ dadhe |
nákiṣ ṭvā ní yamad ā́ suté gamo mahā́ś carasy ójasā ||2||

As a wild elephant rushes on, this way and that way, mad with
heat.
None may restrain thee; yet come hither to the draught: thou
movest mighty in thy power.

avś_20.053.03.

य॒ उग्रः॒ स॒न्न् अ॒निष्टृत स्थिरो॒ र॒णाय सं॒स्कृतः ।
य॒दि स्तोतु॒र् मघ॒वा शृण॒वद् ध॒वं ने॒न्द्रो योषत्य् आ॒ गमत् ॥३॥

yá ugráḥ sánn ániṣṭr̥ta sthiró ráṇāya sáṃskr̥taḥ |
yádi stotúr maghávā śr̥ṇávad dhávaṃ néndro yoṣaty ā́ gamat ||3||

When he, the mighty, ne'er o'erthrown, stedfast, made ready for
the fight.
When Indra, Bounteous Lord, lists to his praiser's call, he will
not stand aloof, but come.
