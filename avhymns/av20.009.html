avś_20.009.01.

तं॒ वो दस्म॒म् ऋतीष॒हम् व॒सोर् मन्दान॒म् अ॒न्धसः ।
अभि॒ वत्सं॒ न॒ स्व॒सरेषु धेन॒व इ॒न्द्रं गीर्भि॒र् नवामहे ॥१॥

táṃ vo dasmám r̥tīṣáham vásor mandānám ándhasaḥ |
abhí vatsáṃ ná svásareṣu dhenáva índraṃ gīrbhír navāmahe ||1||

As cows low to their calves in stalls, so with our songs we
glorify
This Indra, even your wondrous God who checks attack, who
joys in the delicious juice.

avś_20.009.02.

द्युक्षं॒ सुदा॒नुं त॒विषीभिर् आ॒वृतं गिरिं॒ न॒ पुरुभो॒जसम् ।
क्षुम॒न्तं वा॒जं शति॒नं सहस्रि॒णं मक्षू॒ गो॒मन्तम् ईमहे ॥२॥

dyukṣáṃ sudā́nuṃ táviṣībhir ā́vr̥taṃ giríṃ ná purubhójasam |
kṣumántaṃ vā́jaṃ śatínaṃ sahasríṇaṃ makṣū́ gómantam īmahe ||2||

Celestial, bounteous Giver, girt about with might, rich, moun-
tain-like, in precious things
Him swift we seek for foodful booty rich in kine, brought
hundredfold and thousandfold.

avś_20.009.03.

त॒त् त्वा यामि सुवी॒र्यं त॒द् ब्र॒ह्म पूर्व॒चित्तये ।
ये॒ना य॒तिभ्यो भृ॒गवे ध॒ने हिते॒ ये॒न प्र॒स्कण्वम् आ॒विथ ॥३॥

tát tvā yāmi suvī́ryaṃ tád bráhma pūrvácittaye |
yénā yátibhyo bhŕ̥gave dháne hité yéna práskaṇvam ā́vitha ||3||

I crave of thee that hero strength, that thou mayst first regard
this prayer,
Wherewith thou helpest Bhrigu and the Yatis and Praskanva
when the prize was staked.

avś_20.009.04.

ये॒ना समुद्र॒म् अ॒सृजो मही॒र् अप॒स् त॒द् इन्द्र वृ॒ष्णि ते श॒वः ।
सद्यः॒ सो॒ अस्य महिमा॒ न॒ संन॒शे यं॒ क्षोणी॒र् अनुचक्रदे॒ ॥४॥

yénā samudrám ásr̥jo mahī́r apás tád indra vŕ̥ṣṇi te śávaḥ |
sadyáḥ só asya mahimā́ ná saṃnáśe yáṃ kṣoṇī́r anucakradé ||4||

Wherewith thou sentest mighty waters to the sea, that, Indra, is.
thy hero strength.
For ever unattainable is this power of him to whom the worlds.
have called aloud.
