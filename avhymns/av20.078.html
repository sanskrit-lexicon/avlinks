avś_20.078.01.

त॒द् वो गाय सुते॒ स॒चा पुरुहूता॒य स॒त्वने ।
शं॒ य॒द् ग॒वे न॒ शाकि॒ने ॥१॥

tád vo gāya suté sácā puruhūtā́ya sátvane |
śáṃ yád gáve ná śākíne ||1||

Sing this, what time the juice is pressed, to him your Hero
much-invoked,
To please him as a mighty, Bull.

avś_20.078.02.

न॒ घा व॒सुर् नि॒ यमते दानं॒ वा॒जस्य गो॒मतः ।
य॒त् सीम् उ॒प श्र॒वद् गि॒रः ॥२॥

ná ghā vásur ní yamate dānáṃ vā́jasya gómataḥ |
yát sīm úpa śrávad gíraḥ ||2||

He, excellent, withholdeth not his gift of power and wealth in
kine,
When he hath listened to our songs.

avś_20.078.03.

कुवि॒त्सस्य प्र॒ हि॒ व्रजं॒ गो॒मन्तं दस्युहा॒ ग॒मत् ।
श॒चीभिर् अ॒प नो वरत् ॥३॥

kuvítsasya prá hí vrajáṃ gómantaṃ dasyuhā́ gámat |
śácībhir ápa no varat ||3||

May he with might disclose for us the cows' stall, whosesoe'er
it be,
To which the Dasyu-slayer goes.
