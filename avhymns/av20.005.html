avś_20.005.01.

अय॒म् उ त्वा विचर्षणे ज॒नीर् इवाभि॒ सं॒वृतः ।
प्र॒ सो॒म इन्द्र सर्पतु ॥१॥

ayám u tvā vicarṣaṇe jánīr ivābhí sáṃvr̥taḥ |
prá sóma indra sarpatu ||1||

Like women, let this Soma juice invested with its raiment, glide..
Most active Indra, close to thee.

avś_20.005.02.

तुविग्री॒वो वपो॒दरः सुबाहु॒र् अ॒न्धसो स॒दे ।
इ॒न्द्रो वृत्रा॒णि जिघ्नते ॥२॥

tuvigrī́vo vapódaraḥ subāhúr ándhaso sáde |
índro vr̥trā́ṇi jighnate ||2||

Mighty in bulk, strong-necked, stout-armed in the wild rapture.
of the juice
Doth Indra smite the foemen dead.

avś_20.005.03.

इ॒न्द्र प्रे॒हि पुर॒स् त्वं॒ वि॒श्वस्ये॒शान ओ॒जसा ।
वृत्रा॒णि वृत्रहं जहि ॥३॥

índra préhi purás tváṃ víśvasyéśāna ójasā |
vr̥trā́ṇi vr̥trahaṃ jahi ||3||

Indra, advance, go forward thou who by thy might art Lord of
all.
Slay, Vritra-slayer slay thy foes.

avś_20.005.04.

दीर्घ॒स् ते अस्त्व् अङ्कुशो॒ ये॒ना व॒सु प्रय॒छसि ।
य॒जमानाय सुन्वते॒ ॥४॥

dīrghás te astv aṅkuśó yénā vásu prayáchasi |
yájamānāya sunvaté ||4||

Long be thy grasping-hook wherewith thou givest treasure unto-
him
Who pours the juice and worships thee.

avś_20.005.05.

अयं॒ त इन्द्र सो॒मो नि॒पूतो अ॒धि बर्हि॒षि ।
ए॒हीम् अस्य॒ द्र॒वा पि॒ब ॥५॥

ayáṃ ta indra sómo nípūto ádhi barhíṣi |
éhīm asyá drávā píba ||5||

Here, Indra, is thy Soma draught, made pure upon the sacred.
grass.
Run hither, come and drink thereof.

avś_20.005.06.

शा॒चिगो शा॒चिपूजनायं॒ र॒णाय ते सुतः॒ ।
आ॒खण्डल प्र॒ हूयसे ॥६॥

śā́cigo śā́cipūjanāyáṃ ráṇāya te sutáḥ |
ā́khaṇḍala prá hūyase ||6||

Famed for thy radiance, worshipped well! this juice is shed for
thy delight:
Thou art invoked, Ākhandala!

avś_20.005.07.

य॒स् ते शृङ्गवृषो नपात् प्र॒णपात् कुण्डपा॒य्यः ।
न्य् अ॑स्मिन् दध्र आ॒ म॒नः ॥७॥

yás te śr̥ṅgavr̥ṣo napāt práṇapāt kuṇḍapā́yyaḥ |
ny àsmin dadhra ā́ mánaḥ ||7||

To Kundapāyya, grandson's son, grandson of Sringavrish, to
thee,
To him have I addressed my thought.
