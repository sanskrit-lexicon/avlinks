avś_20.103.01.

अग्नि॒म् ईलिष्वा॒वसे गा॒थाभिः शीर॒शोचिषम् ।
अग्निं॒ राये॒ पुरुमील्ह श्रुतं॒ न॒रो ऽग्निं॒ सुदीत॒ये छर्दिः॒ ॥१॥

agním īliṣvā́vase gā́thābhiḥ śīráśociṣam |
agníṃ rāyé purumīlha śrutáṃ náro 'gníṃ sudītáye chardíḥ ||1||

Solicit with your hymns, for aid, Agni the God with piercing
flame,
For riches famous Agni, Purmilha and ye men, Agni to light
our dwelling well.

avś_20.103.02.

अ॒ग्न आ॒ याह्य् अग्नि॒भिर् हो॒तारं त्वा वृणीमहे ।
आ॒ त्वा॒म् अनक्तु प्र॒यता हवि॒ष्मती य॒जिष्ठं बर्हि॒र् आस॒दे ॥२॥

ágna ā́ yāhy agníbhir hótāraṃ tvā vr̥ṇīmahe |
ā́ tvā́m anaktu práyatā havíṣmatī yájiṣṭhaṃ barhír āsáde ||2||

Agni, come hither with thy fires: we choose thee as our Hotai-
priest.
Let the extended ladle full of oil balm thee, best priest, to sit on
sacred grass.

avś_20.103.03.

अ॒छ हि॒ त्वा सहसः सूनो अङ्गिरः स्रु॒चश् च॒रन्त्य् अध्वरे॒ ।
ऊर्जो॒ न॒पातं घृत॒केशम् ईमहे ऽग्निं॒ यज्ञे॒षु पूर्व्य॒म् ॥३॥

ácha hí tvā sahasaḥ sūno aṅgiraḥ srúcaś cáranty adhvaré |
ūrjó nápātaṃ ghr̥tákeśam īmahe 'gníṃ yajñéṣu pūrvyám ||3||

For unto thee, O Angiras, O Son of Strength, move ladles in the
sacrifice,
To Agni, Child of Force, whose locks drop oil, we seek, fore-
most in sacrificial rites.
