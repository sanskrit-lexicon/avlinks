avś_20.080.01.

इ॒न्द्र ज्ये॒ष्ठं न आ॒ भरं ओ॒जिष्ठं प॒पुरि श्र॒वः ।
ये॒नेमे॒ चित्र वज्रहस्त रो॒दसी ओ॒भे॒ सुशिप्र प्राः॒ ॥१॥

índra jyéṣṭhaṃ na ā́ bharam̐ ójiṣṭhaṃ pápuri śrávaḥ |
yénemé citra vajrahasta ródasī óbhé suśipra prā́ḥ ||1||

Bring us, O Indra, name and fame, enriching, mightiest,
excellent,
Wherewith, O wondrous God, fair-cheeked and thunder-armed,
thou hast filled full this earth and heaven.

avś_20.080.02.

त्वा॒म् उग्र॒म् अ॒वसे चर्षणीस॒हं रा॒जन् देवे॒षु हूमहे ।
वि॒श्वा सु॒ नो विथुरा॒ पिब्दना॒ वसो ऽमि॒त्रान् सुष॒हान् कृधि ॥२॥

tvā́m ugrám ávase carṣaṇīsáhaṃ rā́jan devéṣu hūmahe |
víśvā sú no vithurā́ pibdanā́ vaso 'mítrān suṣáhān kr̥dhi ||2||

We call on thee, O King, mighty among the Gods, ruler of men,
to succour us,
All that is weak in us, excellent God, make firm: make our foes
easy to subdue.

Translations compiled by Dr. Mārcis Gasūns.
