avś_20.007.01.

उ॒द् घे॒द् अभि॒ श्रुता॒मघं वृषभं॒ न॒र्यापसम् ।
अ॒स्तारम् एषि सूर्य ॥१॥

úd ghéd abhí śrutā́maghaṃ vr̥ṣabháṃ náryāpasam |
ástāram eṣi sūrya ||1||

Sūrya, thou mountest up to meet the Hero famous for his
wealth,
Who hurls the bolt and works for man:

avś_20.007.02.

न॒व यो॒ नवतिं॒ पु॒रो बिभे॒द बाह्वो॑जसा ।
अ॒हिं च वृत्रहा॒वधीत् ॥२॥

náva yó navatíṃ púro bibhéda bāhvòjasā |
áhiṃ ca vr̥trahā́vadhīt ||2||

Him who with might of both his arms brake nine-and ninety
castles down,
Slew Vritra and smote Ahi dead.

avś_20.007.03.

स॒ न इ॒न्द्रः शिवः॒ स॒खा॒श्वावद् गो॒मद् य॒वमत् ।
उरु॒धारेव दोहते ॥३॥

sá na índraḥ śiváḥ sákhā́śvāvad gómad yávamat |
urúdhāreva dohate ||3||

This Indra is our gracious Friend. He sends us in a full broad
stream
Riches in horses, kine, and corn.

avś_20.007.04.

इ॒न्द्र क्रतुवि॒दं सुतं॒ सो॒मं हर्य पुरुष्टुत ।
पिबा॒ वृषस्व ता॒तृपिम् ॥४॥

índra kratuvídaṃ sutáṃ sómaṃ harya puruṣṭuta |
pibā́ vr̥ṣasva tā́tr̥pim ||4||

Indra, whom many laud, accept the strength-conferring Soma
juice.
Quaff, pour down drink that satisfies.
