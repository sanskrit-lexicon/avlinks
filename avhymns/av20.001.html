avś_20.001.01.

इ॒न्द्र त्वा वृषभं॒ वयं॒ सुते॒ सो॒मे हवामहे ।
स॒ पाहि म॒ध्वो अ॒न्धसः ॥१॥

índra tvā vr̥ṣabháṃ vayáṃ suté sóme havāmahe |
sá pāhi mádhvo ándhasaḥ ||1||

Thee, Indra, we invoke, the Bull, what time the Soma hath been
pressed.
Drink of the sweetly-flavoured juice.

avś_20.001.02.

म॒रुतो य॒स्य हि॒ क्ष॒ये पाथा॒ दिवो॒ विमहसः ।
स॒ सुगोपा॒तमो ज॒नः ॥२॥

máruto yásya hí kṣáye pāthā́ divó vimahasaḥ |
sá sugopā́tamo jánaḥ ||2||

The best of guardian hath the man within whose dwelling-place
ye drink,
O Maruts, giants of the sky.

avś_20.001.03.

उक्षा॒न्नाय वशा॒न्नाय सो॒मपृष्ठाय वेध॒से ।
स्तो॒मैर् विधेमाग्न॒ये ॥३॥

ukṣā́nnāya vaśā́nnāya sómapr̥ṣṭhāya vedháse |
stómair vidhemāgnáye ||3||

Let us serve Agni with our hyms, Sage who consumeth ox and
cow,
Who beareth Soma on his back.
