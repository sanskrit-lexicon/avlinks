avś_20.137.01.

य॒द् ध प्रा॒चीर् अ॒जगन्तो॒रो मण्डूरधाणिकीः ।
हता॒ इ॒न्द्रस्य श॒त्रवः स॒र्वे बुद्बुद॒याशवः ॥१॥

yád dha prā́cīr ájagantóro maṇḍūradhāṇikīḥ |
hatā́ índrasya śátravaḥ sárve budbudáyāśavaḥ ||1||

When, foul with secret spot and stain, ye hastened onward to
the breast.
All Indra's enemies were slain and passed away like froth and
foam.

avś_20.137.02.

क॒पृन् नरः कपृथ॒म् उ॒द् दधातन चोद॒यत खुद॒त वा॒जसातये ।
निष्टिग्र्यः॑ पुत्र॒म् आ॒ च्यावयोत॒य इ॒न्द्रं सबा॒ध इह॒ सो॒मपीतये ॥२॥

kápr̥n naraḥ kapr̥thám úd dadhātana codáyata khudáta vā́jasātaye |
niṣṭigryàḥ putrám ā́ cyāvayotáya índraṃ sabā́dha ihá sómapītaye ||2||

Indra is he, O men, who gives us happiness: sport, urge the
giver of delight to win the spoil.
Bring quickly down, O priests, hither to give us aid, to drink
the Soma, Indra son of Nishtigri.

avś_20.137.03.

दधिक्रा॒व्णो अकारिषं जिष्णो॒र् अ॒श्वस्य वाजि॒नः ।
सुरभि॒ नो मु॒खा करत् प्र॒ ण आ॒यूंषि तारिषत् ॥३॥

dadhikrā́vṇo akāriṣaṃ jiṣṇór áśvasya vājínaḥ |
surabhí no múkhā karat prá ṇa ā́yūṃṣi tāriṣat ||3||

So have I glorified with praise strong Dadhikrāvan, conquering
steed.
Sweet may he make our mouths; may he prolong the days we
have to live.

avś_20.137.04.

सुता॒सो म॒धुमत्तमाः सो॒मा इ॒न्द्राय मन्दि॒नः ।
पवि॒त्रवन्तो अक्षरन् देवा॒न् गछन्तु वो म॒दाः ॥४॥

sutā́so mádhumattamāḥ sómā índrāya mandínaḥ |
pavítravanto akṣaran devā́n gachantu vo mádāḥ ||4||

The Somas very rich in sweets, for which the sieve is destined,
flow,
Effused, the source of Indra's joy. May your strong juices reach
the Gods.

avś_20.137.05.

इ॒न्दुर् इ॒न्द्राय पवत इ॒ति देवा॒सो अब्रुवन् ।
वाच॒स् प॒तिर् मखस्यते वि॒श्वस्ये॒शान ओ॒जसा ॥५॥

índur índrāya pavata íti devā́so abruvan |
vācás pátir makhasyate víśvasyéśāna ójasā ||5||

Indu flows on for Indra's sake—thus have the deities declared.
The Lord of Speech exerts himself, ruler of all, because of
might.

avś_20.137.06.

सह॒स्रधारः पवते समुद्रो॒ वाचमीङ्खयः॒ ।
सो॒मः प॒ती रयीणां॒ स॒खे॒न्द्रस्य दिवे॒दिवे ॥६॥

sahásradhāraḥ pavate samudró vācamīṅkhayáḥ |
sómaḥ pátī rayīṇā́ṃ sákhéndrasya divédive ||6||

Inciter of the voice of song, with thousand streams the ocean
flows,
Even Soma, Lord of Opulence, the friend of Indra, day by day.

avś_20.137.07.

अ॒व द्रप्सो॒ अंशुम॒तीम् अतिष्ठद् इयानः॒ कृष्णो॒ दश॒भिः सह॒स्रैः ।
आ॒वत् त॒म् इ॒न्द्रः श॒च्या ध॒मन्तम् अ॒प स्ने॒हितीर् नृम॒णा अधत्त ॥७॥

áva drapsó aṃśumátīm atiṣṭhad iyānáḥ kr̥ṣṇó daśábhiḥ sahásraiḥ |
ā́vat tám índraḥ śácyā dhámantam ápa snéhitīr nr̥máṇā adhatta ||7||

The black drop sank in Ansumati's bosom, advancing with ten
thousand round about it.
Indra with might longed for it as it panted: the hero-hearted
laid aside his weapons.

avś_20.137.08.

द्रप्स॒म् अपश्यं वि॒षुणे च॒रन्तम् उपह्वरे॒ नद्यो॑ अंशुम॒त्याः ।
न॒भो न॒ कृष्ण॒म् अवतस्थिवां॒सम् इ॒ष्यामि वो वृषणो यु॒ध्यताजौ॒ ॥८॥

drapsám apaśyaṃ víṣuṇe cárantam upahvaré nadyò aṃśumátyāḥ |
nábho ná kr̥ṣṇám avatasthivā́ṃsam íṣyāmi vo vr̥ṣaṇo yúdhyatājáu ||8||

I saw the drop in the far distance moving, on the slope bank of
Ansumati's river,
Like a black cloud that sank into the water. Heroes. I send you
forth. Go, fight in battle.

avś_20.137.09.

अ॒ध द्रप्सो॒ अंशुम॒त्या उप॒स्थे॒ ऽधारयत् तन्वं॑ तित्विषाणः॒ ।
वि॒शो अ॒देवीर् अभ्य् आ॑च॒रन्तीर् बृ॒हस्प॒तिना युजे॒न्द्रः ससाहे ॥९॥

ádha drapsó aṃśumátyā upásthé 'dhārayat tanvàṃ titviṣāṇáḥ |
víśo ádevīr abhy ā̀cárantīr bŕ̥haspátinā yujéndraḥ sasāhe ||9||

And then the drop in Ansumati's bosom, splendid with light,
assumed its proper body;
And Indra with Brihaspati to aid him, conquered the godless
tribes that came against him.

avś_20.137.10.

त्वं॒ ह त्य॒त् सप्त॒भ्यो जा॒यमानो ऽशत्रु॒भ्यो अभवः श॒त्रुर् इन्द्र ।
गूल्हे॒ द्या॒वापृथिवी॒ अ॒न्व् अविन्दो विभुम॒द्भ्यो भु॒वनेभ्यो र॒णं धाः ॥१०॥

tváṃ ha tyát saptábhyo jā́yamāno 'śatrúbhyo abhavaḥ śátrur indra |
gūlhé dyā́vāpr̥thivī́ ánv avindo vibhumádbhyo bhúvanebhyo ráṇaṃ dhāḥ ||10||

Then, at thy birth, thou wast the foeman, Indra, of those the
seven who ne'er had met a rival.
The hidden pair, the heaven and earth, thou foundest, and to
the mighty worlds thou gavest pleasure.

avś_20.137.11.

त्वं॒ ह त्य॒द् अप्रतिमान॒म् ओ॒जो व॒ज्रेण वज्रिन् धृषितो॒ जघन्थ ।
त्वं॒ शु॒ष्णस्या॒वातिरो व॒धत्रैस् त्वं॒ गा॒ इन्द्र श॒च्ये॒द् अविन्दः ॥११॥

tváṃ ha tyád apratimānám ójo vájreṇa vajrin dhr̥ṣitó jaghantha |
tváṃ śúṣṇasyā́vātiro vádhatrais tváṃ gā́ indra śácyéd avindaḥ ||11||

So, Thunder-armed! thou with thy bolt of thunder didst boldly
smite that power which none might equal;
With weapons broughtest low the might of Sushna, and, Indra,
foundest by thy strength the cattle.

avś_20.137.12.

त॒म् इ॒न्द्रं वाजयामसि महे॒ वृत्रा॒य ह॒न्तवे ।
स॒ वृ॒षा वृषभो॒ भुवत् ॥१२॥

tám índraṃ vājayāmasi mahé vr̥trā́ya hántave |
sá vŕ̥ṣā vr̥ṣabhó bhuvat ||12||

We make this Indra very strong to strike the mighty Vritra
dead:
A vigorous Hero shall he be.

avś_20.137.13.

इ॒न्द्रः स॒ दा॒मने कृत॒ ओ॒जिष्ठः स॒ म॒दे हितः॒ ।
द्युम्नी॒ श्लोकी॒ स॒ सोम्यः॒ ॥१३॥

índraḥ sá dā́mane kr̥tá ójiṣṭhaḥ sá máde hitáḥ |
dyumnī́ ślokī́ sá somyáḥ ||13||

Indra was made for giving, set, most mighty, o'er the joyous
draught,
Bright, meet for Soma, famed in song.

avś_20.137.14.

गिरा॒ व॒ज्रो न॒ सं॒भृतः स॒बलो अ॒नपच्युतः ।
ववक्ष॒ ऋष्वो॒ अ॒स्तृतः ॥१४॥

girā́ vájro ná sáṃbhr̥taḥ sábalo ánapacyutaḥ |
vavakṣá r̥ṣvó ástr̥taḥ ||14||

By song, as 'twere, the powerful bolt which none may parry was
prepared:
Lofty, invincible he grew.
