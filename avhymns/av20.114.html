avś_20.114.01.

अभ्रातृव्यो॒ऽअना॒ त्व॒म् अ॒नापिर् इन्द्र जनु॒षा सना॒द् असि ।
युधे॒द् आपित्व॒म् इछसे ॥१॥

abhrātr̥vyó'anā́ tvám ánāpir indra janúṣā sanā́d asi |
yudhéd āpitvám ichase ||1||

O Indra, from all ancient time rivalless ever and companionless
art thou:
In war thou seekest comradeship.

avś_20.114.02.

न॒की रेव॒न्तं सख्या॒य विन्दसे पी॒यन्ति ते सुराश्वः॑ ।
यदा॒ कृणो॒षि नदनुं॒ स॒म् ऊहस्य् आ॒द् इ॒त् पिते॒व हूयसे ॥२॥

nákī revántaṃ sakhyā́ya vindase pī́yanti te surāśvàḥ |
yadā́ kr̥ṇóṣi nadanúṃ sám ūhasy ā́d ít pitéva hūyase ||2||

Thou findest not the wealthy man to be thy friend: those scorn
thee who are flown with wine.
What time thou thunderest and gatherest, then thou, even as a
father, art invoked.
