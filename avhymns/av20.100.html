avś_20.100.01.

अ॒धा ही॒न्द्र गिर्वण उ॒प त्वा का॒मान् महः॒ ससृज्म॒हे ।
उदे॒व य॒न्त उद॒भिः ॥१॥

ádhā hī́ndra girvaṇa úpa tvā kā́mān maháḥ sasr̥jmáhe |
udéva yánta udábhiḥ ||1||

Now have we, Indra, Friend of Song, sent our great wishes
forth to thee.
Coming like floods that follow floods.

avś_20.100.02.

वा॒र् ण॒ त्वा यव्या॒भिर् व॒र्धन्ति शूर ब्र॒ह्माणि ।
वावृध्वां॒सं चिद् अद्रिवो दिवे॒दिवे ॥२॥

vā́r ṇá tvā yavyā́bhir várdhanti śūra bráhmāṇi |
vāvr̥dhvā́ṃsaṃ cid adrivo divédive ||2||

As rivers swell the ocean, so, Hero, our prayers increase thy
might,
Though of thyself, O Thunderer, waxing day by day.

avś_20.100.03.

युञ्ज॒न्ति ह॒री इषिर॒स्य गा॒थयोरौ॒ र॒थ उरु॒युगे ।
इन्द्रवा॒हा वचोयु॒जा ॥३॥

yuñjánti hárī iṣirásya gā́thayoráu rátha urúyuge |
indravā́hā vacoyújā ||3||

With holy song they bind to the broad wide-yoked car the bay
steeds of the rapid God,
Bearers of India, yoked by prayer.
