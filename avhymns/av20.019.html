avś_20.019.01.

वा॒र्त्रहत्याय श॒वसे पृतनाषा॒ह्याय च ।
इ॒न्द्र त्वा॒ वर्तयामसि ॥१॥

vā́rtrahatyāya śávase pr̥tanāṣā́hyāya ca |
índra tvā́ vartayāmasi ||1||

O Indra, for the strength that slays the foe and conquers in the
fight
We turn thee hitherward to us.

avś_20.019.02.

अर्वाची॒नं सु॒ ते म॒न उत॒ च॒क्षुः शतक्रतो ।
इ॒न्द्र कृण्व॒न्तु वाघ॒तः ॥२॥

arvācī́naṃ sú te mána utá cákṣuḥ śatakrato |
índra kr̥ṇvántu vāghátaḥ ||2||

O Indra, Lord of Hundred Powers, may those who praise thee
hitherward.
Direct thy spirit and thine eye.

avś_20.019.03.

ना॒मानि ते शतक्रतो वि॒श्वाभिर् गीर्भि॒र् ईमहे ।
इ॒न्द्राभिमातिषा॒ह्ये ॥३॥

nā́māni te śatakrato víśvābhir gīrbhír īmahe |
índrābhimātiṣā́hye ||3||

O Indra, Lord of Hundred Powers, with all our songs we
invocate.
Thy names for triumph over foes.

avś_20.019.04.

पुरुष्टुत॒स्य धा॒मभिः शते॒न महयामसि ।
इ॒न्द्रस्य चर्षणीधृ॒तः ॥४॥

puruṣṭutásya dhā́mabhiḥ śaténa mahayāmasi |
índrasya carṣaṇīdhŕ̥taḥ ||4||

We strive for glory through the powers immense of him whom
many praise,
Of Indra who supports mankind.

avś_20.019.05.

इ॒न्द्रं वृत्रा॒य ह॒न्तवे पुरुहूत॒म् उ॒प ब्रुवे ।
भ॒रेषु वा॒जसातये ॥५॥

índraṃ vr̥trā́ya hántave puruhūtám úpa bruve |
bháreṣu vā́jasātaye ||5||

For the foe's slaughter I address Indra whom many invocate,
To win us booty in the wars.

avś_20.019.06.

वा॒जेषु सासहि॒र् भव त्वा॒म् ईमहे शतक्रतो ।
इ॒न्द्र वृत्रा॒य ह॒न्तवे ॥६॥

vā́jeṣu sāsahír bhava tvā́m īmahe śatakrato |
índra vr̥trā́ya hántave ||6||

In battles be victorious. We seek thee, Lord of Hundred Powers,
O Indra, that the foe may fall.

avś_20.019.07.

द्युम्ने॒षु पृतना॒ज्ये पृत्सुतू॒र्षु श्र॒वःसु च ।
इ॒न्द्र सा॒क्ष्वाभि॒मातिषु ॥७॥

dyumnéṣu pr̥tanā́jye pr̥tsutū́rṣu śrávaḥsu ca |
índra sā́kṣvābhímātiṣu ||7||

In splendid combats of the hosts, in glories where the fight is
won,
Indra, be victor over foes.
