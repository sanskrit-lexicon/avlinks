उ॒त् तिष्ठ ब्रह्मणस् पते देवा॒न् यज्ञे॒न बोधय ।
आ॒युः प्राणं॒ प्रजां॒ पशू॒न् कीर्तिं॒ य॒जमानं च वर्धय ॥१॥

út tiṣṭha brahmaṇas pate devā́n yajñéna bodhaya |
ā́yuḥ prāṇáṃ prajā́ṃ paśū́n kīrtíṃ yájamānaṃ ca vardhaya ||1||

Rise up, O Brāhmanaspati; awake the Gods with sacrifice.
Strengthen the Sacrificer: aid life, breath, and off-spring, cattle,
fame.

Translations compiled by Dr. Mārcis Gasūns.
