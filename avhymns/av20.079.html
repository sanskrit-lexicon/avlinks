avś_20.079.01.

इ॒न्द्र क्र॒तुं न आ॒ भर पिता॒ पुत्रे॒भ्यो य॒था ।
शि॒क्षा णो अस्मि॒न् पुरुहूत या॒मनि जीवा॒ ज्यो॒तिर् अशीमहि ॥१॥

índra krátuṃ na ā́ bhara pitā́ putrébhyo yáthā |
śíkṣā ṇo asmín puruhūta yā́mani jīvā́ jyótir aśīmahi ||1||

O Indra, give us wisdom as a sire gives wisdom to his sons.
Guide us, O Much-invoked, on this our foray: may we, living,
still enjoy the light.

avś_20.079.02.

मा॒ नो अ॒ज्ञाता वृज॒ना दुराध्यो॑ मा॒शिवासो अ॒व क्रमुः ।
त्व॒या वयं॒ प्रव॒तः श॒श्वतीर् अपो॒ ऽति शूर तरामसि ॥२॥

mā́ no ájñātā vr̥jánā durādhyò mā́śivāso áva kramuḥ |
tváyā vayáṃ pravátaḥ śáśvatīr apó 'ti śūra tarāmasi ||2||

Grant that no mighty foes, unknown, malevolent, unhallowed,
tread us to the ground.
With thine assistance, Hero! may we pass through all the waters
that are rushing down.
