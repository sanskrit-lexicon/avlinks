avś_20.101.01.

अग्निं॒ दूतं॒ वृणीमहे हो॒तारं विश्व॒वेदसम् ।
अस्य॒ यज्ञ॒स्य सुक्र॒तुम् ॥१॥

agníṃ dūtáṃ vr̥ṇīmahe hótāraṃ viśvávedasam |
asyá yajñásya sukrátum ||1||

Agni we choose, the messenger, the herald, master of all wealth,.
Well skilled in this our sacrifice.

avś_20.101.02.

अग्नि॒मग्निं ह॒वीमभिः स॒दा हवन्त विश्प॒तिम् ।
हव्यवा॒हं पुरुप्रिय॒म् ॥२॥

agnímagniṃ hávīmabhiḥ sádā havanta viśpátim |
havyavā́haṃ purupriyám ||2||

With calls they ever invocate Agni, Agni, Lord of the House,
Oblation-bearer, much-beloved.

avś_20.101.03.

अ॒ग्ने देवां॒ इहा॒ वह जज्ञानो॒ वृक्त॒बर्हिषे ।
अ॒सि हो॒ता न ई॒ड्यः ॥३॥

ágne devā́m̐ ihā́ vaha jajñānó vr̥ktábarhiṣe |
ási hótā na ī́ḍyaḥ ||3||

Bring the Gods hither, Agni, born for him who strews the
sacred grass.
Thou art our herald, meet for praise.
