avś_20.043.01.

भिन्धि॒ वि॒श्वा अ॒प द्वि॒षः बा॒धो जही॒ मृ॒धः ।
व॒सु स्पार्हं॒ त॒द् आ॒ भर ॥१॥

bhindhí víśvā ápa dvíṣaḥ bā́dho jahī́ mŕ̥dhaḥ |
vásu spārháṃ tád ā́ bhara ||1||

Drive all our enemies away, smite down the foes who press
around,
And bring the wealth for which we long;

avś_20.043.02.

य॒द् वीला॒व् इन्द्र य॒त् स्थिरे॒ य॒त् प॒र्शाने प॒राभृतम् ।
व॒सु स्पार्हं॒ त॒द् आ॒ भर ॥२॥

yád vīlā́v indra yát sthiré yát párśāne párābhr̥tam |
vásu spārháṃ tád ā́ bhara ||2||

O Indra, that which is concealed in firm strong place
precipitous:
Bring us the wealth for which we long:

avś_20.043.03.

य॒स्य ते विश्व॒मानुषो भू॒रेर् दत्त॒स्य वे॒दति ।
व॒सु स्पार्हं॒ त॒द् आ॒ भर ॥३॥

yásya te viśvámānuṣo bhū́rer dattásya védati |
vásu spārháṃ tád ā́ bhara ||3||

Great riches which the world of men shall recognize as sent by
thee:
Bring us the wealth for which we long,
