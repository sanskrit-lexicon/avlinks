avś_20.119.01.

अ॒स्तावि म॒न्म पूर्व्यं॒ ब्र॒ह्मे॒न्द्राय वोचत ।
पूर्वी॒र् ऋत॒स्य बृहती॒र् अनूषत स्तोतु॒र् मेघा॒ असृक्षत ॥१॥

ástāvi mánma pūrvyáṃ bráhméndrāya vocata |
pūrvī́r r̥tásya br̥hatī́r anūṣata stotúr meghā́ asr̥kṣata ||1||

An ancient praise-song hath been sung: to Indra have ye said
the prayer.
They have sung many a Brihatī of sacrifice, poured forth th,
worshipper's many thoughts.

avś_20.119.02.

तुरण्य॒वो म॒धुमन्तं घृतश्चु॒तं वि॒प्रासो अर्क॒म् आनृचुः ।
अस्मे॒ रयिः॒ पप्रथे वृ॒ष्ण्यं श॒वो ऽस्मे॒ सुवाना॒स इ॒न्दवः ॥२॥

turaṇyávo mádhumantaṃ ghr̥taścútaṃ víprāso arkám ānr̥cuḥ |
asmé rayíḥ paprathe vŕ̥ṣṇyaṃ śávo 'smé suvānā́sa índavaḥ ||2||

In zealous haste the singers have sung forth a song distilling oil
and rich in sweets.
Riches have spread among us, and heroic strength; with us are
flowing Soma drops.
