avś_20.045.01.

अय॒म् उ ते स॒म् अतसि कपो॒त इव गर्भधि॒म् ।
व॒चस् त॒च् चिन् न ओहसे ॥१॥

ayám u te sám atasi kapóta iva garbhadhím |
vácas tác cin na ohase ||1||

This is thine own. Thou drawest near, as the dove turneth to his
mate.
Thou carest too for this our prayer.

avś_20.045.02.

स्तोत्रं॒ राधानां पते गि॒र्वाहो वीर य॒स्य ते ।
वि॒भूतिर् अस्तु सूनृ॒ता ॥२॥

stotráṃ rādhānāṃ pate gírvāho vīra yásya te |
víbhūtir astu sūnŕ̥tā ||2||

O Hero, Lord of Bounties, praised in hymns, may power and
pleasantness
Be his who signs the laud to thee.

avś_20.045.03.

ऊर्ध्व॒स् तिष्ठा न ऊत॒ये ऽस्मि॒न् वा॒जे शतक्रतो ।
स॒म् अन्ये॒षु ब्रवावहै ॥३॥

ūrdhvás tiṣṭhā na ūtáye 'smín vā́je śatakrato |
sám anyéṣu bravāvahai ||3||

Lord of a Hundred Powers, stand up to lend us succour in this
fight:
In others too let us agree.
