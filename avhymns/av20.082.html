avś_20.082.01.

य॒द् इन्द्र या॒वतस् त्व॒म् एता॒वद् अह॒म् ई॒शीय ।
स्तोता॒रम् इ॒द् दिधिषेय रदावसो न॒ पापत्वा॒य रासीय ॥१॥

yád indra yā́vatas tvám etā́vad ahám ī́śīya |
stotā́ram íd didhiṣeya radāvaso ná pāpatvā́ya rāsīya ||1||

If I, O Indra, were the lord of riches ample as thine own,
I should support the singer, God who scatterest wealth! and!
not abandon him to woe.

avś_20.082.02.

शि॒क्षेयम् इ॒न् महयते॒ दिवे॒दिवे राय॒ आ॒ कुहचिद्वि॒दे ।
नहि॒ त्व॒द् अन्य॒न् मघवन् न आ॒प्यं व॒स्यो अ॒स्ति पिता॒ चन॒ ॥२॥

śíkṣeyam ín mahayaté divédive rāyá ā́ kuhacidvíde |
nahí tvád anyán maghavan na ā́pyaṃ vásyo ásti pitā́ caná ||2||

Each day would I enrich the man who sang my praise, in what-
soever place he were.
No kinship is there better, Maghavan, than thine: a father even)
is no more.
