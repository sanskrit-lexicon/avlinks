avś_20.002.01.

मरु॒तः पोत्रा॒त् सुष्टु॒भः स्वर्का॒द् ऋतु॒ना सो॒मं पिबन्तु ॥१॥

marútaḥ potrā́t suṣṭúbhaḥ svarkā́d r̥túnā sómaṃ pibantu ||1||

Let the Maruts drink Trishtups from the Potar's cup, according
to the season Soma from heaven.

avś_20.002.02.

अग्नि॒र् आ॒ग्नीध्रात् सुष्टु॒भः स्वर्का॒द् ऋतु॒ना सो॒मं पिबतु ॥२॥

agnír ā́gnīdhrāt suṣṭúbhaḥ svarkā́d r̥túnā sómaṃ pibatu ||2||

Let Agni from the Kindler's cup drink Trishtups, according to
the season Soma from heaven.

avś_20.002.03.

इ॒न्द्रो ब्रह्मा॒ ब्रा॒ह्मणात् सुष्टु॒भः स्वर्का॒द् ऋतु॒ना सो॒मं पिबतु ॥३॥

índro brahmā́ brā́hmaṇāt suṣṭúbhaḥ svarkā́d r̥túnā sómaṃ pibatu ||3||

Let Indra the Brāhman from the Brāhman's cup drink Trishtups,
according to the season Soma from heaven.

avś_20.002.04.

देवो॒ द्रविणोदाः॒ पोत्रा॒त् सुष्टु॒भः स्वर्का॒द् ऋतु॒ना सो॒मं पिबतु ॥४॥

devó draviṇodā́ḥ potrā́t suṣṭúbhaḥ svarkā́d r̥túnā sómaṃ pibatu ||4||

Let the God, Granter of Wealth, from the Potar's cup drink
Trishtups, according to the season Soma from heaven.
