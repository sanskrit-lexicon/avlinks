तनू॒स् तन्वा॑ मे सहे दतः॒ स॒र्वम् आ॒युर् अशीय ।
स्योनं॒ मे सीद पुरुः॒ पृणस्व प॒वमानः स्वर्गे॒ ॥१॥

tanū́s tanvā̀ me sahe datáḥ sárvam ā́yur aśīya |
syonáṃ me sīda purúḥ pr̥ṇasva pávamānaḥ svargé ||1||

May my self remain in my body: may I enjoy the full time of
life,
Rest thee pleasantly: pour forth abundance, purifying thyself in
Svarga.

Translations compiled by Dr. Mārcis Gasūns.
